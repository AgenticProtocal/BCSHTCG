How It Works

Choose your hero's power source: Tech, Mystic, Cosmic, Bio, or Hybrid. Answer a few questions. AI generates your concept. Submit it to the community gallery. Every week, our artist selects the most promising concepts for professional refinement.

Roadmap

Three phases, not one

Balance the foundation first. Then expand from confidence.

Phase 1: Play (Weeks 1-2)

24 Cards. Live Matches.

Bot goes live. All 24 designed cards loaded. Community plays. Bot auto-tracks 14 balance metrics. First 100+ matches generate initial data.

Phase 2: Balance (Weeks 3-6)

Weekly Tuning Cycle.

Balance Lab starts. Monday: auto-report. Community proposes. Friday: team decides adjustments. Weekend: patched cards go live. Core roster stabilized.

Phase 3: Expand (Week 7+)

New Cards. Your Name.

Card Lab opens. Hero Creation concepts become card designs. Community designs mechanics. New cards enter the bot. Deck building unlocked. Tournaments begin.

We're also developing an AI companion system where every player will have a unique Agent that learns their playstyle. Your contributions now — the way you play, the combos you find, the balance calls you make — will influence how this system works. More details when it's ready to show, not promise.
