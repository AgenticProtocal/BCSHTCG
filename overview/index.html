18 / Why Now + Roadmap

7 years of foundation. The product window is now.

Three things had to converge before this product could exist: original IP with real chain relationships, consumer-ready blockchain infrastructure, and AI agent technology mature enough for persistent companions. All three are ready now — and we built the first two while waiting for the third.

2017-2021: IP & Relationships

Built original characters with 12+ blockchain networks directly. Each hero co-developed with the chain's team. Secured US trademark. This work can't be speed-run — it required years of trust-building during crypto's growth era.

2021-2024: Infrastructure

Built Pentagon Chain (L2) and the Agentic Protocol while the blockchain gaming market burned through hype and crashed. The teams that rushed consumer products in 2021-22 are gone. We built infrastructure instead and survived the cycle.

2024-Now: The Product Window

AI companions became technically viable in 2023-24. Blockchain gaming shed its scam reputation. The TCG market is booming post-Marvel Snap. All three conditions met — now we attach the product to 7 years of assets.

The key insight: We didn't spend 7 years building a product that failed to launch. We spent 7 years building assets (IP, chain relationships, infrastructure, trademark) that are prerequisites for the product — and waited for the market and technology to catch up. The product phase starts now.
