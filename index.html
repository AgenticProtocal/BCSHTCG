How to Play
Deploy. Reveal. Dominate.

Both players deploy cards face-down to 3 battlefield Nodes, then reveal simultaneously. No waiting for your opponent's turn. Every moment is shared tension.

01
🎯
Deploy

Choose which heroes to send to each Node. Your opponent does the same — in secret. 6 turns of escalating Bitzen energy means bigger plays as the match progresses.

02
⚡
Reveal

Cards flip simultaneously. Both players see what happened at the same time. The surprise, the outplay, the read — you experience it together. No one waits.

03
🏆
Dominate

Control 2 of 3 Nodes by Power after Turn 6. SYNC at any time to double the stakes — your opponent accepts, counters, or retreats. Poker meets card games.

SYNC Mechanic

Either player can double the stakes at any time. Accept the SYNC, counter-SYNC to quadruple, or retreat. Creates moments of pure drama that are made for streaming.

Simultaneous Reveal

No waiting for your opponent. Both players deploy and reveal together. Every match is a shared experience — the tension, the surprise, the outplay.

Fair to Play

Higher rarity = flashier art, never stronger stats. Buy cosmetics, not power. Reach any rank without spending a dollar.

Each battlefield Node has a unique modifier that changes the strategy. Adapt your deployment every game. No two matches feel the same.

The World
Welcome to BTC1

A planet powered by Bitszen energy — so powerful it attracted the Nexarchs, cosmic conquerors who assimilate species into the Hivemantle collective. When they invaded, ordinary citizens awakened with extraordinary powers. They became the heroes.

The Heroes

BCSH Corp — a coalition of powered warriors, each with a unique fighting style. Aiko's immovability. Ethan's adaptability. Nova's speed. Frost's disruption. 7 roles from Bruiser to Trickster to Evolver. Your squad, your identity.

The Nexarchs

The Hivemantle army — an assimilation force so relentless most civilizations don't believe in them until it's too late. Corruption weakens heroes. Hivemantle stacking means enemy forces grow stronger together. The more Nexarchs you deploy, the deadlier they become.
